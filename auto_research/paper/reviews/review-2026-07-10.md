### W3 — Full=Router mechanism clarified (FIXED in v5)
**Location:** §5.3

### W4 — Sign-advantage paper (arxiv:2605.07689) creates a gap (NEW this week)
**Location:** Section 5.4 / Table 3

An adversarial reviewer may ask: "why not use sign-advantage? This would likely fix your 73% zero-variance problem entirely." We mention it as a queued remedy (§7) but have no experimental results with it. The gap is now visible and reviewers know this paper.  
**Fix:** EXP-108 (sign-advantage, priority 9, ~3h on A800) — highest-impact pending experiment. If A800 restores, run EXP-108 before EXP-099.
